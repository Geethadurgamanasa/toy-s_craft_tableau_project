ABOUT THE PROJECT

ToyCraft Tales: An Insightful Analysis of Toy Manufacturers in the United States

ToyCraft Tales is a data-driven analytical project focused on exploring the toy manufacturing industry in the United States. Using interactive Tableau visualizations, the project analyzes year-wise performance and state-wise distribution of toy manufacturers to identify trends, patterns, and industry insights.

The objective of this project is to transform raw manufacturing data into meaningful visual stories that help understand market behavior, regional strengths, and growth opportunities within the toy industry. This analysis supports informed decision-making through clear and intuitive dashboards.
